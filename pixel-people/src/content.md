# Pixel People

## Home

### Summary (Hero)

Pixel People is the first NFT collection made by its community. Each Pixel Person acts as a membership to exclusive clothing and NFT drops. You can collect, customize, and sell each Pixel Person or their features (eyes, nose, hair, etc.) individually.
Most NFT collections don’t give enough freedom to their communities or flexibility to their NFTs. Pixel People fixes this by giving you the freedom to make your Pixel Person how you want it.

[cool header with some Pixel People in a pixel world]

Don't know what an NFT is? Don't worry, we can get you up to speed.

### How it works (how to mint)

1. Connect your wallet
   In the top right corner of the website, you can click the wallet icon to connect your wallet. Once connected, you will be able to mint your Pixel People.

2. Go to "Mint" and select features
   Select features that add to your previous selection. Some features are different prices to encourage rarity. Once you're finished, you can mint your Pixel Person!

3. Buy! Buy! Buy!
   Join the Pixel People community by minting your first Pixel Person. Next, you can break, build, customize, or swap Pixel People features. See below for more information.

### Features (what can a Pixel Person do?)

Break them apart! Your Pixel Person breaks apart into its features.
Customizable! Change the way your Pixel Person looks with different features.
Build from features! Get all of the necessary features to make a Pixel Person from scratch.
Swap features! Multiple Pixel People can swap features with each other.

## Roadmap

Pixel People launches. Only 10,000 male human Pixel People are available to mint by hand-picking features. Our community grows and diversifies.

The next 10,000 Pixel People are only available by making them from scratch.

Pixel People launches 10,000 female human Pixel People in the same fasion as the male Pixel People. Then another 10,000 made from scratch.

Anyone can make ideas for new features and publicly vote on them. Feature drops will add more features to the Pixel People marketplace.

Pixel People tame the wildlife and pets come into their lives.

Pixel People colonize Planet Pixel. Female and male Pixel People can have children and create a family tree together.

The Pixel People become an interplanetary species. They meet new alien species on their journey.

Clothing? Video Game? New innovations to NFTs? Who knows... Make sure to join the community to find out!
